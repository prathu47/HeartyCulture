Our impact in Kanha Shanti Vanam

With over 150+ years of collective experience and expertise in fields ranging from Forestry, Plantation, Plant Bio Technology to Plant Physiology and Environmental Engineering. the Heartyculture Nursery team has been recognised with multiple awards from the Government of Telangana (Green Kanha and Mayuri Haritha Vanam). Kanha Shantivanam has been awarded the Platinum Honour for being 'The Biggest Green Campus' from Indian Green Building Council in 2019.
